Product 01 / Finance Fixer

Cleaner financial records. Clearer review.

Finance Fixer helps organize transactions, receipts, paystubs, bank activity, recurring expenses, spending patterns, and tax-adjacent records into cleaner views for personal review or professional preparation.

Organizational and analytical software. Not legal, tax, accounting, investment, or financial advice.
Transaction organizationGroup financial activity by category, vendor, time period, and review path.
Spending pattern analysisIdentify recurring charges, high-frequency vendors, budget trends, and activity worth closer review.
Receipt and paystub supportBring supporting records into a more reviewable structure.
Advisor-ready viewsPrepare cleaner records for financial planners, tax preparers, budget coaches, or personal review.
Product 02 / APEX

Professional review systems with structure.

APEX turns scattered professional inputs into structured reviews, coaching reports, development plans, scoring models, redaction workflows, and clearer performance conversations.

APEX supports review and development workflows. It does not guarantee employment, promotion, compensation, business, or career outcomes.
Coaching workflow engineOrganize coaching inputs, discussion themes, follow-ups, and professional development actions.
Performance review supportStructure notes, feedback, patterns, strengths, gaps, and evidence into clearer review materials.
Scoring and analysis modulesUse review models to compare signals, identify gaps, and prioritize next steps.
Redaction and report preparationPrepare sensitive or professional materials for cleaner review and structured reporting.
Manager, coach, and self-review use casesSupport development conversations without reducing complex people decisions to simplistic answers.
