Product 01

Finance Fixer

Cleaner Financial Records. Clearer Financial Decisions.

Finance Fixer is a personal finance and transaction analysis tool that helps individuals and advisors organize messy financial activity into clearer records, categories, and patterns.

Financial data is rarely clean. Transactions come from multiple banks, cards, receipts, paystubs, statements, spreadsheets, and personal notes. Finance Fixer is built to help bring that activity into a more usable structure, making it easier to understand where money is going, what records may matter, and which patterns deserve a closer review.

The software is designed as an organizational and analytical layer for personal finance workflows. It can help identify spending categories, recurring expenses, budget trends, receipt records, paystub details, bank activity, and tax-adjacent documentation that may be useful for review with a qualified professional.

Key Features
Transaction OrganizationConsolidate and review financial activity with cleaner structure, better categorization, and more useful record grouping.
Spending Pattern AnalysisIdentify budget patterns, recurring charges, high-frequency vendors, and areas where activity may need closer attention.
Receipt and Record SupportOrganize receipts, paystubs, bank records, and related financial documents into a more reviewable format.
Tax-Adjacent RecordsSurface transactions and documentation that may be relevant for tax preparation review without making tax determinations.
Advisor-Ready ViewsHelp individuals, planners, preparers, and coaches work from cleaner records instead of scattered financial data.
Use Cases
  • Personal Financial Cleanup: Turn months or years of messy activity into a more organized view of spending, income, recurring costs, and budget behavior.
  • Advisor or Tax Preparer Preparation: Prepare cleaner records before working with a financial planner, tax preparer, budget coach, or other professional.
  • Budget and Cash Flow Review: Identify recurring expenses, spending categories, and patterns that may affect household budgeting or planning conversations.
Finance Fixer is an organizational and analytical tool and does not provide legal, tax, investment, accounting, or financial advice.
Bring your financial records into focus with software built for cleaner analysis, better preparation, and more informed conversations.
Product 02

APEX

Structured Review for Professional Decisions.

APEX is professional decision-support and coaching workflow software for structured analysis, performance review, development planning, and practical workplace decision-making.

Professional decisions often require more than notes, instinct, or a scattered spreadsheet. APEX is built to help users review information through structured workflows, scoring models, redaction tools, reports, and coaching-oriented analysis. It supports a more disciplined review process for performance conversations, career planning, productivity improvement, and professional development.

The product is designed for serious users who need clarity, documentation, and repeatable review methods. APEX can help organize inputs, evaluate patterns, generate structured reports, support coaching modules, and guide professional analysis without reducing complex decisions to a single simplistic answer. It is built to support better thinking, not to guarantee outcomes.

Key Features
Structured WorkflowsReview professional information through consistent frameworks instead of fragmented notes or informal judgment.
Coaching ReportsGenerate clear reports that support coaching conversations, professional review, and development planning.
Scoring ModulesUse structured scoring and evaluation tools to compare information, identify gaps, and prioritize next steps.
Redaction SupportPrepare sensitive or professional materials for cleaner review workflows with appropriate separation of information.
Development ToolsSupport coaching, self-review, workflow improvement, and professional growth through guided analysis modules.
Use Cases
  • Professional Performance Review: Organize performance inputs, identify development themes, and create clearer coaching conversations.
  • Career and Workflow Planning: Review professional goals, work habits, productivity patterns, and decision points through a structured process.
  • Manager or Coach Support: Help managers, coaches, or advisors prepare more consistent reports, recommendations, and review materials.
APEX supports structured professional analysis and coaching workflows but does not guarantee employment, promotion, compensation, or career outcomes.
Use APEX to bring structure, evidence, and clarity to professional decisions that deserve more than a loose review.
